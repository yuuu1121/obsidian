## 확률 질량 함수 (Probability Mass Function, PMF)

---
### Properties of PMF

  >$$\begin{align}(1)\quad& 0\le P(X=x)\le1,\qquad x\in D\\\\(2)\quad& \sum\limits_{x\in D}P(X=x)=1\end{align}$$
