# Gibb's Inequality
- **Definition**
	- [[Maximum Likelihood Estimation#An Interpretation of MLE KL-Matching|KL-Divergence]] ([[Entorpy|relative entropy]])가 항상 0 이상임을 보장하는 부등식
	  $$D_{\text{KL}}(p \parallel q) = \int p(x) \log \frac{p(x)}{q(x)} dx \geq 0$$
